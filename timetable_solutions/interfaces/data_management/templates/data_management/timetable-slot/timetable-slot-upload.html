    Required file structure
    slot_id day_of_week starts_at ends_at relevant_year_group_ids
    1 1 09:00 09:45 1; 2
    • slot_id*: A positive integer (1, 2, 3, ...) used to identify this timetable slot. Note that these must be unique
    • day_of_week*: Day of week the slot is on. Please use 1, 2, ... 7 to represent Monday, Tuesday, ..., Sunday
    • starts_at*: When the slot starts. Give the time in for HH:MM as shown in the example table.
    • ends_at*: When the slot starts. Give the time in for HH:MM as shown in the example table
    • relevant_year_group_ids*: IDs of the year groups this slot is relevant to. Separate each id with a semicolon as shown in the example table. Note that each year group can have its own timetable structure, or you can make all of your slots "relevant" to all year groups by including all your year group ids in this column, separated by semicolons. Note that no two timetable slots that are relevant to a year group are allowed to overlap. Similarly, a timetable slot and a break relevant to a year group cannot overlap.
    • *required - no empty values are allowed in these columns
